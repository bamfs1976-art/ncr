Monte Carlo season simulator, adapted from SportsMetrics. Each run plays out all 36 fixtures using team ratings with home advantage and random match variance, then the two conference winners meet in the Grand Final. Results from rounds 1 and 2 and any scores you have entered in the Predictor are locked into every run.

Season simulation

Model: expected margin = rating difference plus 2.5 home advantage, match variance is normally distributed (sd 13). Win chance uses a logistic curve on the expected margin. Try bonuses are sampled from the winning margin. Fiji's July "home" games in Cardiff, Liverpool and Edinburgh give home advantage to the opposition; neutral venues carry none.
